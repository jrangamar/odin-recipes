Garlic Bread

Description

Ingredients
  • Any kind of firm, thin, sliced bread
  • Garlic-infused olive oil
  • Garlic salt
Directions
  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Place sliced bread on a baking tray lined with aluminum foil
  3. Generously coat bread slices with garlic-infused olive oil.
  4. Season with garlic salt, to taste
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes until crunchy throughout.
